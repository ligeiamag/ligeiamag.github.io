About Us

“Of all the women whom I have ever known, she, the outwardly calm, the ever-placid Ligeia, was the most violently a prey to the tumultuous vultures of stern passion.”

from “Ligeia,” by Edgar Allan Poe

Based out of Baltimore, Ligeia is a literary magazine publishing unconventional voices. We believe in the writer’s strange obsessions, and we are obsessed with strange writers.

Ligeia publishes poetry, short fiction, nonfiction, artwork, and interviews in online quarterly issues. We embrace works of the modern gothic—absurd, grotesque, eccentric, derelict.

Work published in Ligeia has been selected for the Pushcart Prize. We also nominate for Best of the Net and other awards. We look forward to reading your work.
