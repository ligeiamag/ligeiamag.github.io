Mission Statement

“Of all the women whom I have ever known, she, the outwardly calm, the ever-placid Ligeia, was the most violently a prey to the tumultuous vultures of stern passion.”

from “Ligeia,” by Edgar Allan Poe

Ligeia, from the Greek ligys, meaning clear-voiced, is a literary magazine promoting overlooked works and bringing new and upcoming writers to light. To foster the talent of all writers is to fashion the future of writing, in all of its vibrancy. Ligeia seeks voices beyond the conventional to showcase the diversity of the writing community.

Ligeia believes in the strangeness and the obsessions of the writer.

Based in Baltimore, Maryland, Ligeia publishes poetry, short fiction, nonfiction, artwork, and interviews in quarterly issues.
