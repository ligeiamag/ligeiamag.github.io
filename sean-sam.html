Sean Sam
Fiction & Nonfiction Editor

Sean Sam is a writer and web designer whose work has previously appeared in Potomac Review, Bird's Thumb, and Red INK among other places. He's a member of the Navajo tribe. He lives in Maryland and can be found online at www.seansam.com.
