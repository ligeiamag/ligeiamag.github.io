Sean Sam
Fiction & Nonfiction Editor

Sean Sam's writing has appeared or is forthcoming in The Malahat Review, The Westchester Review, Salt Hill, ellipsis…literature and art, Déraciné, Potomac Review, Bird’s Thumb, and Red INK. He is the winner of Terrain.org’s 12th Annual Fiction contest and has received an honorable mention in Zoetrope: All-Story’s Short Fiction Competition. Find him online at www.seansam.com.
