Ashley Wagner
Poetry Editor

Ashley Wagner is a poet, editor, and essayist whose work has previously appeared in Grub Street. She enjoys reading all forms of creative writing, from poetry to creative non-fiction to genre-bending short stories. She is a Maryland native and tweets @ashley_dawn_w.
