On Acceptance

Ligeia acquires first electronic rights and the non-exclusive right to include your work in our archives. We ask that you credit Ligeia with first publication of your work. We remind you that by submitting to Ligeia you are affirming that you are the author of the work and that it has not been published in any print or electronic form including on personal web pages. If this is not the case, please let us know now. Upon publication, rights revert to the author.
