Ligeia is open for submissions from October 17 to November 21, 2020.

Send all submissions to editors@ligeiamagazine.com as an attachment (doc, docx, rtf, txt, or jpg).

Simultaneous submissions are fine. Let us know if your work is accepted elsewhere. We don't accept previously published work.

Please submit to only one category at a time.

Include a third-person bio of up to 50 words in the body of the email.

Use the following as an email subject line template: Category - Author Name
example: Fiction - Jane Doe

Category Guidelines
Fiction Send fiction up to 2,000 words. Multiple flash pieces in a single file are fine, but please ensure they don't exceed 2,000 words combined. Our tastes are eclecticâ€”we enjoy literary and genre fiction in equal measure. We recommend reading past issues to familiarize yourself with the work we like to publish.
Poetry Send 1-5 poems together in a single document. We're looking for descriptive, immersive works—with a touch of strange to them.
Nonfiction Send nonfiction up to 2000 words as an attachment. We accept creative nonfiction and reviews.
Art Please send art as a .jpg, .jpeg, or .png.

On Acceptance

Ligeia acquires first electronic rights and the non-exclusive right to include your work in our archives. Upon publication, rights revert to the author. Unfortunately, we are unable to pay contributors at this time. In the future, if your work appears in other publications, we ask that you credit Ligeia as the original publisher.
