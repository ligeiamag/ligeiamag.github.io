Matt Lee
Fiction & Nonfiction Editor

Matt Lee is an actor, teacher, and writer based in Maryland. His work has been featured at SURFACES, Tragickal, and Occulum, among other venues. He has produced numerous original works for the stage with Maryland Ensemble Theatre. He tweets @Gallows_Ticket. For more info visit neutralspaces.co/matt_lee/.
