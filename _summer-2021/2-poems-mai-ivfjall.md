## Glossolalia
<div class="stanza">
<p class="poemline">the bees are dying—can you feel it? i want</p>
<p class="poemline">to press my tongue gently against green mesh i want</p>
<p class="poemline">to bite softly into a sponge of any color i want</p>
<p class="poemline">to rip & chew & spit that sponge bit out of my mouth</p>
<p class="poemline">when it becomes small & hard & no longer satisfying the rose</p>
<p class="poemline">is a rat the rat is a rose</p>
<p class="poemline">saturn is rising in the 8th house</p>
</div>
<div class="stanza">
<p class="poemline">reading about emotional responses to the end of nature</p>
<p class="poemline">i start to feel like i might become a rain drop i wish</p>
<p class="poemline">i could give birth to an orchid as my body moves</p>
<p class="poemline">in one language & thinks in another—another landscape</p>
</div>
<div class="stanza">
<p class="poemline">another tongue—i see dogs wearing amber necklaces</p>
<p class="poemline">& think whoa, amber is the color of my energy</p>
<p class="poemline">pain is a psalm that sings your body is a bivouac</p>
</div>
