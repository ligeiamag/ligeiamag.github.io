Christine Yates
Poetry Editor

With a pre-med degree and M.S. in professional writing, Christine Yates is a lab rat dissecting literary bodies while wondering, “how can inanimate words generate a soul?”

She’s attracted to impactful female writers like Maya Angelou, Mary Oliver, and Margaret Atwood—artists that birth immersive yet economic descriptions with attention to word sounds. She is currently a writing intern with The Pop Topic, specializing in health-related news articles.
